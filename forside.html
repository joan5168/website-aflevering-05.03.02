About

We go for wild, local, gluten-free, ethically sourced, seasonal and plant-based ingredients, that bring a little bit of nature in our busy lives.

we are a India restaurant that stands for healthy options, we love to take what nature is giving us. One of the main goals of the manifesto is to work in close dialogue with local producers, and to innovate and improve the overall quality of our food

Our food and cold-pressed juices are 100% organic, pure, fresh, and unpasteurized, leaving their nutritive power intact. Rise and Curry is based on the simple belief that food should be good for you, good for the planet, and delicious.
